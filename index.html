SURF = Surfacing Unused Realities Forum.

In these times of avoiding the inevitable, we try to connect in digital ways. We hope you will join us in order to create new ways of communicating. We are all on the same page and 'the other' is not existing anymore. Let's make this event an example of nearness in remoteness.
